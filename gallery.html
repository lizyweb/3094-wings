Wings Activities

Wings Trust is dedicated to uplifting the lives of those in need of a compassionate shoulder to lean on. We focus on improving the quality of life for the economically disadvantaged, with a special emphasis on widows, deserted women, single-parent children, and orphans. Our mission is to provide support and opportunities for these vulnerable groups, ensuring they have the resources and care to lead fulfilling lives. Join us in making a meaningful difference.
